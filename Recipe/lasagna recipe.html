Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

3/4 lb. lasagna noodles, 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling, 1 lb. ground beef, 2 cloves minced garlic, 1 tsp. dried oregano, Kosher salt, Freshly ground black pepper, 1 (32-0z.) jar marinara, 16 oz. whole milk ricotta, 1/2 c. freshly grated Parmesan, 1/4 c. chopped parsley, plus more for garnish, 1 1/2 lb. sliced mozzarellah

135 calories per square of lasagna

Takes 1/45 min to cook

Can have as many servings as you like

It is hard to make

Steps

Step 1:

Preheat oven to 375 F.

Step 2:

In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta according to package directions until al dente, less than 2 minutes.

Step 3:

Drain and drizzle a bit of olive oil to prevent noodles from sticking together.

Step 4:

Meanwhile, in a large pot over medium-high heat, heat oil.

Step 5:

Cook ground beef until no longer pink, breaking up with a wooden spoon, then drain fat.

Step 6:

Return beef to skillet and add garlic and oregano and stir for 1 minute.

Step 7:

Season with salt and pepper, then add marinara and stir until warmed through.

Step 8:

Combine ricotta, 1/4 cup Parmesan, and parsley in a large mixing bowl and season with salt and pepper. Set aside.

Step 9:

In a large casserole dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, a single layer of lasagna noodles, a layer of ricotta mixture, and a single layer of mozzarella, then repeat layers.

Step 10:

Top last layer of noodles with meat sauce, Parmesan, and mozzarella.

Step 11:

Cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes, then increase temperature to 400 F and bake uncovered for 18 to 20 minutes.

Step 12:

Garnish with parsley and serve.
